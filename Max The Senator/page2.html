Max Krawarik

United States Senator for Rhode Island

Climate Change & The Environment

I support, and will fund, actions to combat climate change worldwide. We can do this in Rhode island by expanding Rhode Island’s role in the transition to renewable energy, particularly offshore wind.

Defense & Foregin Affairs

As Rhode Island is the first line of defense against Atlantic attack, I will ensure that funding for the U.S. Navy’s shipbuilding programs remains strong, and that the Naval Undersea Warfare Center is a continued priority, while also advocating for fiscal responsibility in defense spending.
